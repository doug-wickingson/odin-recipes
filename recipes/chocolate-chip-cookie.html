Chocolate Chip Cookie

A delicious dunkable chocolate cookie that is delicious anytime.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • Coarse sea salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl mix together the flour, baking soda and the salt
  3. In a large bowl beat together the butter, sugars, vanilla extract and the vinegar until smooth. Beat in the egg just until combined.
  4. Mix the flour mixture into the wet mixture just until combined. Stir in the chips.
  5. Using a large cookie scoop drop about 3 inches apart onto the cookie sheet.
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 mins, they will look light brown around the edges and will look underdone in the center.
