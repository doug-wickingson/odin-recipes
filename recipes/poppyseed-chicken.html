Poppyseed Chicken

Description

Poppy Seed Chicken and Grape Pasta Salad

In this delicious pasta salad you'll find tender bowtie pasta, hearty pieces of chicken, sweet grapes, crisp celery and craisins all coated with creamy poppy seed dressing.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz. bow tie pasta
  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast
  • 12 oz. red grapes
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 3/4 cups craisins
  • 1/2 cup green onions
  • 1/3 cup granny smith apples

Steps

  1. Cook pasta to al dente in salted boiling water.
  2. Drain then rinse with cold water and drain completely. Set aside and let cool.
  3. In a mixing bowl whisk dressing ingredients
  4. Add drained pasta to a bowl along with chicken, grapes, celery, apples, green onions and craisins.
  5. Pour dressing over top and toss to evenly coat. Season with salt to taste.
  6. Chill for 30 min before serving. Serve chilled.
